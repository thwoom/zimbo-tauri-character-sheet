How to Use ZimboMate

Saving and Loading Characters

  1. Click Export/Import in the toolbar
  2. Choose Export to save your current character (e.g., "my-hero.json")
  3. To load later, reopen Export/Import, select Import, and pick your saved file

Customizing Your Experience

Open the Settings panel from the toolbar to access:

  • Theme Selection - Switch between cosmic, classic, or moebius themes
  • Auto XP on Miss - Enable automatic XP gain when rolls fail, or track manually

Dice Rolling

  1. Select your dice type and modifiers
  2. Click roll to see results with animations
  3. View roll history and automatic XP tracking
  4. Critical hits and failures trigger special visual effects

Inventory Management

  1. Add items to your inventory with type and properties
  2. Click to equip/unequip gear (visual indicators show equipped status)
  3. Consume items to reduce quantity or remove them entirely
  4. View calculated totals for armor and weapon damage
