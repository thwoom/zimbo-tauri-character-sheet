ZimboMate

Your companion from a broken future
Track your character's journey through time. Your wounds, your victories, your possessions—all preserved in digital echoes. Every scar tells a story of battles fought across timelines, every item carries the weight of multiple realities. Built with Tauri + React for a fast, cross-platform experience that bridges the gap between the digital and the physical, allowing you to maintain your existence across the void of forgotten time.
Time bends, but I do not.

Core Features

Wound Tracking

Your flesh remembers what your mind forgets. Track HP, XP, level, bonds, debilities, and status effects with real-time updates and visual indicators. Every scar tells a story of battles fought across time, every wound a lesson learned in the school of hard knocks. Monitor your character's condition with color-coded status indicators that speak of your resilience against the forces of entropy. The system remembers what you have forgotten, preserving the echoes of every near-death experience.

Probability Engine

The dice fall as they must. Roll with modifiers, view comprehensive history, and automatically apply XP on misses. Each failure teaches lessons written in experience, each success a moment of defiance against the chaos of probability. Features critical hit/failure animations and sound effects that echo through time, making each roll a visceral reminder of your mortality and your capacity to overcome it. The system tracks every decision, every risk taken, every moment of courage in the face of uncertainty.

Paradoxical Inventory

Equip, consume, or drop items with automatic calculation of armor and weapon damage. Visual indicators show equipped status and item types across multiple timelines. Each item exists in a state of quantum uncertainty until observed, its properties shifting between realities. The interface calculates the impossible, tracking weapons that have not yet been forged and armor that has already failed in a thousand timelines. Your possessions carry the weight of multiple existences, each one a fragment of a reality that has already ended.

Memory Crystals

Take persistent notes with compact and full modes. Each session preserved in digital amber, maintaining detailed logs for campaign continuity across the void. Your experiences crystallize into permanent records, each word a memory of something that has not yet happened. Switch between views seamlessly, maintaining the illusion of continuity in a universe where nothing truly endures. The notes you write now have already been read in the future, creating a paradox of knowledge that defies the linear flow of time.

Temporal Reversal

Reverse recent actions with a built-in undo system. Step backward through your timeline, though the scars of what might have been remain in the code. Never lose progress due to accidental clicks or misclicks during intense gameplay. The system allows you to rewrite moments that have already occurred, though the memory of what might have been lingers in the digital ether. Each reversal is a small act of defiance against the inexorable march of entropy.

Timeline Preservation

Save or load characters with the ExportModal. Backup your existence to files that exist outside of time, share them with others who walk the same path. Export your character's data to JSON files that can be shared or backed up, ensuring your digital self persists across the void. Import characters from other timelines, merging fragments of existence into a coherent whole. Your data exists in a state of quantum superposition until observed, each save a snapshot of a reality that may or may not come to pass.

Technical Architecture

Tauri

Cross-platform desktop framework using Rust for the backend and web technologies for the frontend. Provides native performance with web flexibility, creating a bridge between the digital and physical worlds.

React

Modern UI framework for building responsive, component-based interfaces. Manages the state of your character's existence across multiple timelines with efficient rendering and virtual DOM.

TypeScript

Type-safe JavaScript that catches errors before they can corrupt your character's data. Ensures the integrity of your digital self across the void of forgotten time.

Tailwind CSS

Utility-first CSS framework for rapid UI development. Provides the visual language of the cyber-barbarian aesthetic, with custom properties for the color scheme and glass morphism effects.

Vite

Fast build tool and development server that accelerates the development process. Ensures your character's interface loads quickly across all timelines.

Vitest

Unit testing framework that validates the integrity of your character's data and ensures the reliability of the probability engine across multiple realities.

Item System

The inventory system supports various item types, each with their own properties and behaviors:

Weapons Armor Consumables Magic Items Materials Gear

Each item type has specific behaviors and visual indicators. Weapons automatically calculate damage, armor provides protection, consumables can be used for healing or buffs, magic items have special properties, materials are used for crafting, and gear provides utility bonuses. The system tracks equipped status and provides visual feedback for item interactions.
